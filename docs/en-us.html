Versions:

  • Private alpha version "α (PRIVATE)":
    v0.x-alpha
  • Public beta version "β (PUBLIC)":
    v1.x-beta
  • Commercial gamma versions "γ (COMMERCIAL)" - available on demand:
    v2.x-release

α (PRIVATE) REPOSITORY:

  • Description: Private development projects, still in research, testing, or personal use phases.
  • Access: Only @MuriloKrominski or specific collaborators have access.
  • Usage: Not ready or available to the public.

β (PUBLIC) REPOSITORY:

  • Description: Projects that are Open Source or partially available to the public.
  • Access: Public access, anyone can clone and contribute.
  • Usage: Public collaboration or Open Source projects, documented to be easily understood or replicated.

γ (COMMERCIAL) REPOSITORY:

  • Description: Projects that were, may be, or will be implemented in a commercial environment. These may have a public beta version, but the complete or modified version is commercialized.
  • Access: Private repositories, or partially public, but full access is reserved for clients.
  • Usage: Includes projects commissioned by companies, closed solutions, and products sold on demand.
